Noise Pollution Tracker

Welcome to NoiseTracker!

Are you concerned about the impact of noise pollution on your community's health and well-being? You're not alone. With NoiseTracker, we empower you to record, analyze, and visualize noise levels in your local area.

Here's How It Works

  1. Detect Noise: Click the 'Start Recording' button and let NoiseTracker do its work. It records ambient noise in your vicinity for a few seconds.
  2. Analyze Volume: As you record, NoiseTracker analyzes the volume of the detected noise and displays both the current and peak volume levels.
  3. Playback the Noise: If you wish, you can listen back to the recorded noise by clicking the 'Play' button.
  4. Plot the Noise on a Map: To visualize noise pollution in your area, enter a location and hit 'Submit Location'. This will display it on the map. You can then 'Place' a marker on the map which represents the peak noise level at that location.

Use NoiseTracker as a tool to raise awareness about noise pollution, to mobilize local action, or to inform local policy and planning decisions. We all deserve quieter, healthier communities. NoiseTracker is a step in that direction.

Please Note: This application requires your permission to access your microphone for recording purposes. We respect your privacy. Your data is used only within the app and is not stored or shared.
